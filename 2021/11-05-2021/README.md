<h1 align="center">
The Internet Inequality
</h1>
There is a large discrepancy in the percentage of people per county with access to fixed terrestrial broadband at speeds of 25 Mbps/3 Mbps as of the end of 2017. On average,98.9% of people in Connecticut have access to broadband. In contrast, only 56.8% of people living in Arkansas can say the same.
